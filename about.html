The Vision

FitPulse Pro was born from a simple observation: fitness apps were either too generic or too clunky. We wanted to build a "Software as a Service" that felt like a professional diagnostic tool—one that users would actually pay for because of its precision and beauty.

Developed as part of the BTS DAI Frontend Project, this application leverages modern CSS (Tailwind v4) and high-performance JavaScript to deliver a seamless, responsive experience across all devices.
